PUBLICATIONS

The book is a true story about entrepreneurship and confronting failure. Aman the protagonist is a 15-year-old boy with entrepreneurial dreams. He starts his first venture which is an online education platform for high school students. His venture fails to break even and it shuts down. He is not deterred by his failure. Instead, he goes on to launch another venture which fails too. This book describes his struggles and perseverance in starting one venture after another without being discouraged by failure. It tells about his typical Indian family and their dreams of an engineer son, dropping out of college and his belief in making it big. He finally manages to start a pizzeria which he manages to run for a few years. He has to shut it down due to unforeseen circumstances. At the end after many unsuccessful startups, he has created a T-shirt with changeable print designs which he has named ‘T-share’. He gives up his secure job and returns again to chase after his dreams. He believes Failure needs to be successful always but he needs to succeed only once to defeat Failure.
